Aml Artistry

About the Artist

For as long as Aml can remember, art has been a strong passion of hers. Even though many children grow up painting and drawing, creating art was a hobby that remained even as she got older. While studying Business Management in 2017, she decided to take the initiative to expand her art skills and see where it might take her. She started by painting Arabic Calligraphy, and was later inspired to explore different mediums and art styles such as oil paintings, portraits, landscapes, and abstract art.

In 2019, she was given the opportunity to step out of her comfort zone and paint murals in various establishments in the GTA, where she gained interest in a different art style. She appreciates art because it allows her to express her emotions and she finds inspiration through the people around her and everything she has experienced.

FAQ

  • How much do I commission you for a painting?

    If you would like to commission me for a painting, send me an email describing what you would like and attach a reference photo if applicable. If you have you have a specific medium you’d like me to use, please state it along with the size of the canvas. Please describe what you would like in as much detail as you can!

  • How do I commission you for a mural?

    Please send me an email with information regarding the location and what you would like, along with when you want it to be completed. Depending on the location, I will be able to take a look at the establishment and give you an accurate quote.

  • How do you receive your payments?

    Once you have decided that you would like to commission me, I take a 50% non-refundable deposit for the painting, and the rest once you say you like how the painting has come out.

  • How will I receive my painting?

    Depending on your location I will either personally deliver it to you, or ship it to you. I will let you know how the painting will be delivered when we communicate.

  • What are your rates?

    My rates will vary depending on the subject and the size of the canvas. There is no set price, due to the variations of subjects I paint. Please email me a short description and the size of a quote.
